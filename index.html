KOREAN SKINCARE

Korean Skincare Routine

Korean skincare is one of my favorite beauty topics because it focuses on healthy, glowing skin. Instead of covering skin problems with heavy makeup, Korean skincare teaches people how to take care of their skin step by step. The routine is gentle, consistent, and focused on hydration.

A typical Korean skincare routine includes cleansing, toning, moisturizing, and protecting the skin with sunscreen. Many products contain natural ingredients like green tea, snail mucin, aloe vera, and rice extract. These ingredients help calm, hydrate, and brighten the skin. I like Korean skincare because it feels relaxing and makes my skin look fresh and healthy.

Basic Korean Skincare Steps

  1. Oil Cleanser
  2. Foam Cleanser
  3. Toner
  4. Serum or Essence
  5. Moisturizer
  6. Sunscreen
